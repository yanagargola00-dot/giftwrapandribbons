Privacy

Okay, here's a rewrite of that privacy policy to make it sound more human:

**1. Intro**

Your privacy rocks, and we're serious about keeping your info safe – both yours and your pet's! This explains how we handle your data when you're using our pet care thing.

**2. What Info We Grab**

*   **If You're a Pet Parent:**

    *   Your name, email, number, and login stuff.
    *   All about your pet: name, age, breed, health stuff, and what they like to eat.
    *   Appointment times, reminders, and chats with the vet.
    *   What you buy – food, meds, subscriptions, etc.
*   **Payment Stuff:**

    *   Credit card or whatever you use to pay (it's all handled safely by the payment people).
    *   Your past payments.
*   **Techy Stuff:**

    *   Your IP address, what kind of device you're using, browser, etc.
    *   How you use the app/site.
    *   Cookies and other tracking things.
*   **Optional Stuff:**

    *   Your feedback, surveys, or questions you send us.

**3. How We Use Your Info**

We use your info to:

*   Set up and manage your account and your pet's profile.
*   Book your appointments and remind you about them.
*   Take care of orders, payments, and subscriptions.
*   Talk to vets, suppliers, or our support team.
*   Suggest products or services you might like.
*   See how the platform is doing and make it better.
*   Stop bad guys from messing with the platform.

**4. Who We Share With**

We might share data with:

*   **Vets:** Just enough info to manage appointments and give advice.
*   **Payment People:** To safely process your payments.
*   **Delivery Folks:** So they can bring you your stuff.
*   **Analytics People:** To see how the platform's doing.
*   **Cops (if needed):** If the law makes us.

We *never* sell your personal or pet's info.

**5. Cookies and Tracking**

We use cookies and tracking to:

*   Keep you logged in.
*   Remember your pet, orders, and preferences.
*   Suggest stuff you might like.
*   See how the app/site works.

You can control cookies in your browser or app settings.

**6. How Long We Keep Your Data**

We keep your account, pet, and payment info as long as your account is open, or as long as we need to for legal or business reasons. You can ask us to delete your account and data.

**7. Your Rights**

You can:

*   See, change, or delete your personal and pet data.
*   Cancel subscriptions or appointments.
*   Stop getting promo emails or notifications.
*   Manage your cookie settings.

**8. How We Protect Your Data**

We use encryption, secure servers, and other security stuff to protect your info. But no system is perfect, so keep your login info safe.

**9. Kids' Privacy**

Our platform isn't for kids under 13. We don't knowingly collect their data.

**10. Policy Changes**

We might update this policy sometimes. We'll post any changes here with the new date.
